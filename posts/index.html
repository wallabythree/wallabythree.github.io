MCH2022 badge challenge: “Hack Me If You Can”

2022-08-01
The badge for the MCH2022 hacker camp comes with a CTF challenge, which, thanks to the ESP32’s Xtensa architecture, appears to be somewhat protected against classic stack overflow attacks. However, thanks to recursion in the main loop, a buffer overflow is all that is needed to solve this challenge. This post will walk through the vulnerability and explain how it can be exploited to steal the flag.
